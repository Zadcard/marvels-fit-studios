# Marvel Fit Studios - Operational Platform Valuation & System Presentation

## Executive Summary & Purpose

This presentation deck has been engineered specifically for **software valuation, system pricing, and commercial evaluation assessment**. It presents Marvel Fit Studios as a custom, high-value, turn-key operational management platform — explicitly distinguishing it from generic websites or basic CRUD templates.

---

## Slide Deck Structure (10 High-Impact Slides)

1. **Title & System Identity**: Marvel Fit Studios — Operational & Financial Management Operating System (Prepared for System Pricing Assessment).
2. **Executive Summary**: Unified Operating System replacing fragmented tools (spreadsheets, WhatsApp, paper receipts) into one central workspace.
3. **Operational Workspaces**: Admin Command Center & Coach Floor Workspaces with role-tailored permissions and security boundaries.
4. **Lead Pipeline**: Structured prospect acquisition funnel (New → Trial Booked → Trial Done → Won/Lost) with loss reason analytics.
5. **Member Dossiers & Clinical Safety**: 360-degree client context with real-time clinical injury warning badges (ACL, meniscus, lumbar strains) across admin & coach views.
6. **Schedule & Attendance**: Chronological Agenda, Week Cards, and Hourly Grid views with automated Cairo live session indicators (🔴 Live Now, Upcoming, Ended) and granular attendance states.
7. **Subscription Lifecycle**: Flexible membership plans, session balance auto-decrementing, pause/resume/cancel/renew controls, and auditable digital PDF receipts.
8. **Financial Operations**: Real-time cash-in & cash-out ledgers, payment method breakdown (InstaPay, Visa, Cash), date-range filtering, and one-click CSV export.
9. **Technical Architecture**: Next.js 16.2 App Router, React 18, TypeScript 5, Supabase PostgreSQL with RLS, Auth.js JWT authentication, transactional RPC functions, Vitest & Playwright test suites.
10. **Commercial Valuation Assets**: Bespoke turn-key product, proprietary IP & custom design system, ready for multi-branch studio expansion or SaaS licensing.
